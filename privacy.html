Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

At Raghuvir Consultants, we understand that confidentiality and privacy are paramount when dealing with your financial life. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

To provide accurate fiduciary advice, we collect personal and financial information, which may include:

  • Identity Information (Name, PAN, Aadhaar, DOB)
  • Contact Information (Email, Phone, Address)
  • Financial Information (Income, Assets, Liabilities, Existing Portfolios)
  • Risk Profile Assessment Data

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly for the purpose of providing investment advisory services, which includes:

  • Creating customized financial plans and asset allocation strategies.
  • Conducting SEBI-mandated risk profiling and KYC compliance.
  • Communicating portfolio reviews, recommendations, and administrative updates.
  • Billing and fee calculation.

3. Data Security & Storage

We implement robust physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. Client data is stored securely on encrypted servers. We retain your data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by SEBI regulations (typically 5 years post-termination of agreement).

4. Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

We may share necessary data with third-party service providers (such as Smallcase or compliance auditors) solely for the purpose of facilitating your investment operations or meeting regulatory requirements. All third parties are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, review, and request correction of your personal data held by us. You may also withdraw your consent for data processing at any time, subject to legal or regulatory restrictions which may impact our ability to continue providing advisory services.
